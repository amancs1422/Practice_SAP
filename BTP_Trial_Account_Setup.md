# BTP Trial Account Setup
## :clipboard: Create a SAP Business Technology Platform(BTP) Trial Account.
### Step 1 : Access sap.com site and login/create an SAP account.
![](https://github.com/amancs1422/Practice_SAP/blob/9d8e512e1c95fea65d21cd86eb4eba0d408ba6af/Images/BTP_Trial_Setup_1.jpg)
### Step 2 : Fill in the required details and acknowledge the Terms and Conditions, Click on Submit to create the SAP account.
![](https://github.com/amancs1422/Practice_SAP/blob/9d8e512e1c95fea65d21cd86eb4eba0d408ba6af/Images/BTP_Trial_Setup_2.jpg)
### Step 3 : In SAP Account search for BTP and Try Trial.
![](https://github.com/amancs1422/Practice_SAP/blob/9d8e512e1c95fea65d21cd86eb4eba0d408ba6af/Images/BTP_Trial_Setup_3.jpg)
### Step 4 : Once Trial Account is created click on the button shown below to access trial account.
![](https://github.com/amancs1422/Practice_SAP/blob/9d8e512e1c95fea65d21cd86eb4eba0d408ba6af/Images/BTP_Trial_Setup_4.jpg)
### Step 5 : You will be able to view your global account and trial sub account on the Account explorer Homepage.
![](https://github.com/amancs1422/Practice_SAP/blob/9d8e512e1c95fea65d21cd86eb4eba0d408ba6af/Images/BTP_Trial_Setup_5.jpg)
